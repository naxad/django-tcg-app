How to photograph your card (for best results)

  • Fill the frame with the card; keep edges fully visible.
  • Shoot straight on (not at an angle) in bright, even light.
  • Avoid glare or heavy shadows; wipe the sleeve if dusty.
  • Take both front & back photos.
